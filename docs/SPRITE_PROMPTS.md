# SPRITE PROMPTS - Living Creatures in Habitats

> **IMPORTANT**: These are LIVING CREATURES, not objects with faces. They have personality, arms, expressions, and exist in habitats.

---

## AMZN (Box Creature)

### STATE 4/5 (UP):
```
Create a 64x64 pixel art sprite scene of a cute living cardboard box CREATURE in a floating habitat.

CHARACTER:
- A living cardboard box creature - it has LIFE, not just a box
- Cute stubby arms/flaps on the sides (like the box flaps are little arms)
- Two big friendly dot eyes on the front, happy expression
- The Amazon smile-arrow is its MOUTH (smiling!)
- Slightly bouncy pose, full of energy
- Warm tan/brown cardboard body (#D4A574)
- Floating with bobbing animation feel, small shadow beneath

ENVIRONMENT:
- Small grassy or dark platform underneath
- Tiny tape roll buddy nearby
- 4-5 GREEN UP ARROWS (▲) floating upward around it
- Happy, energetic atmosphere
- Small sparkles

STYLE:
- Pokemon/Tamagotchi creature style
- Cute, alive, has personality
- 12-15 colors max
- Transparent background
```

### STATE 2 (DOWN):
```
Create a 64x64 pixel art sprite scene of a sad living cardboard box CREATURE in a floating habitat.

CHARACTER:
- A living cardboard box creature - it has LIFE, not just a box
- Droopy stubby arms/flaps on the sides hanging down sadly
- Two big worried dot eyes on the front, sad expression
- The Amazon smile-arrow is its MOUTH (frowning/flat)
- Slouched, tired pose, low energy
- Muted tan/brown cardboard body (#B8956A)
- Floating lower, larger shadow beneath

ENVIRONMENT:
- Small dark platform underneath
- Tiny tape roll buddy nearby looking worried
- 4-5 ORANGE DOWN ARROWS (▼) falling around it
- Gloomy, tired atmosphere
- No sparkles

STYLE:
- Pokemon/Tamagotchi creature style
- Sad, droopy, but still alive
- 12-15 colors max
- Transparent background
```

## STATE VARIATIONS GUIDE

### UP States (4, 5):
- Energetic, bouncy pose
- Big happy eyes
- Arms/appendages raised or active
- Bright, saturated colors
- GREEN UP ARROWS (▲) floating upward
- Lots of sparkles and glow
- Small shadow (floating high)

### DOWN States (1, 2):
- Droopy, tired pose
- Sad or worried eyes (half-closed)
- Arms/appendages hanging down
- Muted, desaturated colors
- ORANGE or RED DOWN ARROWS (▼) falling
- No sparkles, dim glow
- Larger shadow (floating low)

### NEUTRAL State (3):
- Calm, relaxed expression
- Normal colors
- NO arrows
- Peaceful floating
- Gentle glow
